Eastern Tales is an experiment in type based blog design, with a very minimal use of graphics. It heavily relies on type sizes, color and the ubiqutously available Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif and Georgia, Times, Serif font groups with an occassional interspersion of Verdana! To make the design usable by a vast group of users, there is no Javascript or fancy apps. :)
